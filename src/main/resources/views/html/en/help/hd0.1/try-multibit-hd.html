Packed with useful features

MultiBit HD is a major upgrade to Classic and contains a huge number of useful features requested by Classic users. Here is a short summary, but the link provides a more complete list including technical information:

  • no more worrying about losing private keys or passwords - use your "wallet words" (seed phrase) to restore them
  • simple and intuitive user interface - big accessible controls each with a clear purpose
  • Trezor support out of the box - just plug it in
  • detailed integrated Help if you get stuck
  • fast, lightweight direct block chain download just like before
  • private Contacts database with private notes and tags
  • private Payments database with integrated exchange rates
  • adjustable fees - wide range to suit your needs
  • adjustable units - support for bits, ubits, millis etc
  • integrated exchange rates from multiple exchanges to support national currencies
  • export transactions with historical spot rates to a financial package in standard CSV format - easier accounting
  • themes to suit different lighting conditions
  • better language coverage than Classic

MultiBit HD is not free

Each spend incurs an extra 1,000 satoshi fee (about 0.5 US cents) that is bundled up and sent to the MultiBit development team every 15 to 25 sends.

You can find out more by following the link.

MultiBit HD and Classic can run together on the same machine

MultiBit HD has been designed to be completely separate from Classic. They can both safely operate on the same machine without interfering with each other.

Therefore you can explore MultiBit HD and if you like it you can make the decision to spend into your MultiBit HD wallet until you have made the transition in a manner comfortable to you.

Download MultiBit HD

You can download MultiBit HD from the front page. Just read and accept the terms and conditions (they tell you about the fee) then click the button corresponding to your operating system. After a moment the download will begin and some installation instructions will appear.
